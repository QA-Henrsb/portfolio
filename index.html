About

QA Engineer with 5+ years delivering quality for web, mobile, and SaaS products across international companies. I specialize in both manual and automated testing — Functional, Regression, Smoke, API, and End-to-End — to ensure reliable releases throughout the software development lifecycle.

I have strong hands-on experience with Playwright, Cypress, Postman, and REST APIs, building and maintaining scalable test automation while supporting CI/CD pipelines with GitHub Actions and Jenkins. I validate web apps, mobile apps (Android & iOS), APIs, admin portals, integrations, and production environments.

I investigate production issues using Sentry, Firebase, PostHog, AWS, SQL, log analysis, and HAR files to perform root-cause analysis and support engineering teams in delivering stable releases. I'm passionate about continuous improvement and leverage AI-assisted tools to accelerate test design, defect investigation, documentation, and quality workflows.
